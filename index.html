BMR & TDEE Settings
How TDEE is calculated (no double counting): Your BMR is multiplied by a clean sedentary baseline of 1.2 only. Your actual movement is then added separately and explicitly — step NEAT (steps × 0.045 kcal) plus estimated training burn from each logged session. This means all activity comes from what you actually log, never estimated twice. The baseline multiplier above defaults to 1.2 and should only be changed if you specifically want a different resting baseline.
